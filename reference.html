Upgrades; TDUT

Introduction

Upgrades are the main method of furthering progress in TDUT. Beginning with a free upgrade on Island 1, they gradually increase in requirements over time, with many variations and specific traits along the way. It should be noted that in the game's code, and on the text of UP16, upgrades (commonly abbreviated as UP) are written out as 'SK' for CN (Island 1 ~ 3), 'UF' for Class 0 (Island 5 ~ 7), and ‘JP’ for Class 1. (Void) The main upgrades on islands 1-3 are all CLN upgrades, and those on islands 5-7 are Class 0 upgrades, Void (or Jumper Abyss) have Class 1, or Joyful.

Upgrade Types, Classes, & Examples[]

There are 7 Types of Upgrades: Normal, Permanent, Buyable, Choice, Secret, Parkour, and Extension. Normal Upgrades does not save at Reset Layers and does not require parkours or something. Permanent Upgrades are opposite of normal and saves, but still does not require parkours or any other things. Buyable Upgrades are a upgrade that can bought infinite times, and also doesn't save. also they work different, like (1.1 ^ bought count) and not multiply like (1.1 * bought count) so, its effect goes skyrocket when bought many times. but, if you bought an Upgrade that saves its bought state, only it saves and bought count does NOT save. Choice Upgrades are upgrades that if you buy a upgrade that's also Choice Upgrade with it, you can't buy the other upgrade that was Choice Upgrade with it. Secret Upgrades, does save even if you did Reset Layers. as the name, it is mostly hidden, and it can be unlocked by other main upgrades like Exist 1, and more. also, its price is Free. Parkour Upgrades, also saves like Permanent. they also need other main upgrades, but this time, it needs Parkour skills, and also its Free. Finally, Extension Upgrades are unlocked after buying UP173 (Ifinitude). They are pretty much the same as normal upgrades.
